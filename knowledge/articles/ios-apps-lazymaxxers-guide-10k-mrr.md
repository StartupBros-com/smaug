A step-by-step playbook for building profitable iOS apps with minimal effort, focused on TikTok ad-driven growth to a hard paywall.

## Core Framework

**VIRAL FEATURE + CORE HUMAN DESIRE = $$$**

Every successful app targets a deep emotional insecurity or desire (attractiveness, health, wealth, status). The app must have a "reveal" moment that can be screen-recorded and turned into a TikTok ad. Examples cited: Cal AI (food scanning, $3M+/mo), UMax (face ratings), Pray Screen.

## Key Steps

1. **Study what's working** — study top apps' onboarding and paywall flows. Don't clone; extract the pattern and improve on it.
2. **Hard paywall** — no trial, no freemium. Pricing: $12.99/week + $59.99/year. Retention offer at $44.99/year (shown only on cancel — Apple-approved method).
3. **Before/after ad format** — record the "reveal" action, use z-image (fal.ai) to generate AI faces, SwapTok for face-swap videos, TikTok Studio to assemble. 10–12 variants in an hour, no UGC creators needed.
4. **TikTok Smart+ campaign** — optimize for subscribe/purchase events, NOT installs. $50/day, USA only. Run 2–3 days per creative.
5. **Scale thresholds** — CPI < $2.00, CTR > 1.00%, CPA < LTV (~$30+ per paying user). Increase budget +30% every 3 days max.

## Cheat Codes

- TikTok matches first $6,000 in ad spend on new accounts (doubles distribution)
- Apple Small Business Program: cuts fee from 30% to 15%
- Apple payouts take 45 days — plan cashflow accordingly

## Ad Rules

- Ads must look native (like organic TikToks, not ads)
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- "Comment bait" to drive engagement and cheaper reach
- Delete comments mentioning cost ("it costs money")
